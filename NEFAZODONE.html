Mechanism of Action

Nefazodone functions as a dual-action antidepressant, blocking serotonin reuptake while simultaneously antagonizing 5-HT2A receptors. This dual mechanism helps increase serotonin availability while reducing some of the side effects associated with excessive 5-HT2A activation. The medication also has mild antagonist activity at alpha-1 adrenergic receptors and histamine H1 receptors. Its metabolite, meta-chlorophenylpiperazine (mCPP), contributes to its therapeutic effects through additional serotonergic activity.
