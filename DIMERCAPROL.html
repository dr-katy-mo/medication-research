Mechanism of Action

Dimercaprol functions as a bidentate chelating agent, with its two sulfhydryl groups forming coordinate covalent bonds with heavy metals including arsenic, mercury, lead, and gold. The resulting metal-dimercaprol complexes are water-soluble and readily excreted through the kidneys.
