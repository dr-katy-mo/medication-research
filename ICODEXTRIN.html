## 2. THERAPEUTIC CONTEXT

### Mechanism of Action
Icodextrin functions as a colloid osmotic agent in peritoneal dialysis, creating natural osmotic gradients for ultrafiltration. It is slowly absorbed and metabolized through normal carbohydrate pathways.

### Clinical Utility

  • Primary use in peritoneal dialysis
  • Particularly effective for long-dwell exchanges
  • Better ultrafiltration profile compared to glucose solutions
  • Generally well-tolerated with good safety profile
