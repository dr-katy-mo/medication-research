Scopolamine functions as a non-selective muscarinic acetylcholine receptor antagonist with particular affinity for central nervous system receptors. The medication modulates the natural balance between cholinergic and other neurotransmitter systems, particularly in areas controlling vestibular function, memory formation, and autonomic regulation.
