3. REGULATORY AND PRECEDENT CONTEXT

Current Status

Nitazoxanide is FDA-approved (2002) for treatment of diarrhea caused by Cryptosporidium parvum and Giardia lamblia in pediatric and adult patients. It is available as both prescription tablets (Alinia) and oral suspension. The medication has regulatory approval in multiple countries including Mexico, where it was first developed and approved for broader indications including viral hepatitis.
