Duvelisib selectively inhibits PI3K-δ and PI3K-γ isoforms, which are predominantly expressed in hematopoietic cells. Duvelisib is FDA-approved for treating relapsed or refractory chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL), small lymphocytic lymphoma (SLL), and follicular lymphoma after at least two prior systemic therapies.
