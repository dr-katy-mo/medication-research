Mechanism of Action

Belinostat inhibits histone deacetylases through chelation of zinc ions in the enzyme active sites via its hydroxamic acid group. This inhibition leads to accumulation of acetylated histones, resulting in chromatin relaxation and transcriptional activation of previously silenced genes. The mechanism affects multiple cellular pathways including cell cycle arrest, differentiation, and apoptosis.
