2. THERAPEUTIC CONTEXT

Mechanism of Action

Chlorpheniramine polistirex functions as a selective H1 histamine receptor antagonist. It competitively binds to peripheral H1 receptors, blocking the action of endogenous histamine and preventing allergic symptoms including rhinorrhea, sneezing, pruritus, and urticaria. The polistirex resin provides extended release over 8-12 hours, maintaining consistent therapeutic levels. The medication has minimal anticholinergic effects compared to other first-generation antihistamines.

Clinical Utility

Primary therapeutic applications include treatment of seasonal and perennial allergic rhinitis, allergic conjunctivitis, and mild allergic skin reactions. It is particularly useful for nighttime dosing due to its extended-release formulation and mild sedating properties that can aid sleep in allergic patients. The medication has a well-established safety profile with over 70 years of clinical use. It is considered appropriate for both temporary use during acute allergic episodes and longer-term management of chronic allergic conditions.
