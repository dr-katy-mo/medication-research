# Naturopathic Formulary Medication Evaluation Protocol
# LANREOTIDE Evaluation

## 1. NATURAL DERIVATION ASSESSMENT

### Source Investigation
Lanreotide is a synthetic octapeptide analog of natural somatostatin, a hormone naturally produced in the hypothalamus and pancreatic delta cells. While not directly extracted from natural sources, it was developed by mimicking the structure and function of endogenous somatostatin.

### Structural Analysis

  • Core structure: Synthetic octapeptide
  • Closely mirrors somatostatin's active site
  • Contains modified amino acid sequences to improve half-life
  • Cyclic structure similar to natural somatostatin
  • Enhanced stability compared to natural somatostatin while maintaining biological activity

### Biological Mechanism Evaluation

  • Binds to natural somatostatin receptors (primarily SSTR2 and SSTR5)
  • Integrates with endogenous growth hormone regulation pathways
  • Mimics natural somatostatin's inhibitory effects on hormone secretion
  • Works through physiological feedback mechanisms

### Natural System Integration

  • Supports natural hormone regulation
  • Works through existing endocrine pathways
  • Longer half-life than natural somatostatin (enhanced physiological effect)
  • Maintains natural feedback loops in hormone regulation

## 2. THERAPEUTIC CONTEXT

### Mechanism of Action

  • Binds to somatostatin receptors
  • Inhibits GH, IGF-1, and various peptide hormone secretion
  • Reduces secretion of pancreatic and gastrointestinal hormones
  • Anti-proliferative effects on certain tumor cells

### Clinical Utility

  • Primary indication: Acromegaly
  • Treatment of gastroenteropancreatic neuroendocrine tumors
  • Long-acting formulation (Somatuline Depot)
  • Monthly administration
  • Used in adult patients

### Integration Potential

  • Can be integrated with dietary modifications
  • Complements lifestyle interventions for hormone regulation
  • May be combined with other natural approaches for endocrine support

## 3. REGULATORY AND PRECEDENT CONTEXT

### Current Status

  • FDA approved
  • Prescription only
  • Available as injectable depot formulation
  • Classified as a synthetic peptide hormone analog

### Comparable Medications

  • Octreotide (similar somatostatin analog)
  • Natural alternatives: Limited direct alternatives
  • Complementary approaches: Dietary modification, stress management
