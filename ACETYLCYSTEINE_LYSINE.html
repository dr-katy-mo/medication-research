ACETYLCYSTEINE LYSINE is derived from natural sources. Lysine is an essential amino acid that must be obtained from dietary sources including meat, fish, dairy products, and certain plant proteins. Both components have been used in clinical medicine for decades, with NAC having particular historical significance in traditional medicine practices involving sulfur-containing compounds.
