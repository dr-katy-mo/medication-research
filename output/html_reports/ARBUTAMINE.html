Clinical Utility

Arbutamine was developed specifically for pharmacological stress testing in patients unable to undergo exercise stress testing. It provided a controlled method for inducing cardiac stress to evaluate coronary artery disease and myocardial function. Additionally, arbutamine was withdrawn from the market in the early 2000s due to commercial reasons rather than safety concerns.
