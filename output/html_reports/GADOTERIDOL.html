### Mechanism of Action Gadoteridol functions as a paramagnetic contrast agent for magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). The gadolinium ion shortens T1 and T2 relaxation times of nearby water protons, creating enhanced contrast in areas where the agent accumulates. The macrocyclic chelation structure provides stability and reduces free gadolinium ion release.
