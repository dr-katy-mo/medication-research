### Mechanism of Action Netarsudil dimesylate works as a dual-mechanism inhibitor targeting both Rho-associated protein kinase (ROCK) and norepinephrine transporter (NET). ROCK inhibition leads to relaxation of the trabecular meshwork and increased conventional outflow of aqueous humor.
