Clinical Utility

Maribavir is indicated for treatment of post-transplant cytomegalovirus (CMV) infection/disease that is refractory to conventional antiviral therapy (ganciclovir, valganciclovir, foscarnet, or cidofovir) in adult and pediatric patients. It provides an important therapeutic option for multidrug-resistant CMV infections, particularly in immunocompromised patients where CMV can cause life-threatening complications.
