### Mechanism of Action Viltolarsen functions as an exon-skipping therapy by binding to specific sequences in dystrophin pre-mRNA. Through complementary base pairing, it masks the splice sites of exon 53, causing the cellular splicing machinery to skip this exon during mRNA processing.
