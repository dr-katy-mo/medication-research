### Mechanism of Action Sparsentan functions as a selective antagonist of both AT1 and ETA receptors. It modulates the binding of angiotensin II to AT1 receptors, preventing vasoconstriction, aldosterone release, and pro-inflammatory signaling. Simultaneously, it blocks endothelin-1 binding to ETA receptors, reducing vasoconstriction, inflammation, and fibrosis.
