BROMOCRIPTINE is produced through modification of natural compounds, preserving the essential natural framework while enhancing therapeutic properties. Bromocriptine is a semi-synthetic derivative of ergot alkaloids, specifically derived from ergotamine, which occurs naturally in the fungus Claviceps purpurea (ergot fungus). This fungus grows parasitically on rye and other cereal grains. The ergot alkaloids have been recognized since medieval times, though their therapeutic properties were first systematically studied in obstetrics and migraine treatment.
