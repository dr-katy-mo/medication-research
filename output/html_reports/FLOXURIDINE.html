### Mechanism of Action Floxuridine functions as a cytotoxic antineoplastic agent through inhibition of DNA synthesis. After intracellular conversion to active metabolites, it irreversibly binds to thymidylate synthase, preventing formation of thymidine triphosphate required for DNA replication.
