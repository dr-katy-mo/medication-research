Clinical Utility

Irinotecan is primarily indicated for treatment of metastatic colorectal cancer, either as first-line therapy in combination with fluorouracil and leucovorin, or as second-line monotherapy after fluorouracil failure. It is also used in combination regimens for other solid tumors including lung, gastric, and pancreatic cancers.
