Clinical Utility

Primary applications include short-term relief of nasal congestion due to allergic rhinitis, common cold, or sinusitis. Also used as an ophthalmic decongestant for minor eye irritation. The medication provides rapid onset of action (5-10 minutes) with duration of 4-8 hours. Critical limitation includes rebound congestion (rhinitis medicamentosa) with prolonged use beyond 3-5 days.
