### Mechanism of Action Latanoprostene bunod functions as a dual-mechanism intraocular pressure (IOP) lowering agent. After topical administration, it is hydrolyzed to release latanoprost acid and butanediol mononitrate. Latanoprost acid binds to prostaglandin F receptors, increasing aqueous humor outflow primarily through the uveoscleral route.
