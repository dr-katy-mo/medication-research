Clinical Utility

Fenfluramine was primarily used as an appetite suppressant for weight loss, often in combination with phentermine ("fen-phen"). Additionally, it was withdrawn from the U.S. market in 1997 due to associations with serious cardiovascular complications, including pulmonary hypertension and cardiac valvular disease.
