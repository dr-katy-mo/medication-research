Nabilone is primarily indicated for chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting (CINV) that has not responded adequately to conventional antiemetics. It serves as a second-line treatment option for cancer patients experiencing severe treatment-related nausea and appetite loss. Clinical studies demonstrate efficacy comparable to or exceeding conventional antiemetics, with a generally well-tolerated side effect profile consisting mainly of cannabinoid-related effects like drowsiness and dizziness.
