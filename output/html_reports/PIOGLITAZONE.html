### Mechanism of Action Pioglitazone functions as a PPARγ agonist, binding to and activating this nuclear receptor in insulin-sensitive tissues including adipose tissue, skeletal muscle, and liver. Upon activation, PPARγ forms heterodimers with retinoid X receptors and binds to peroxisome proliferator response elements in gene promoters. The net effect is improved insulin sensitivity, reduced hepatic glucose production, and enhanced peripheral glucose uptake.
