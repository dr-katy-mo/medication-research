2. THERAPEUTIC CONTEXT

### Mechanism of Action Perflutren functions as an ultrasound contrast agent through acoustic enhancement. When administered intravenously as lipid-encapsulated microbubbles, it increases the echogenicity of blood, improving visualization of cardiac structures and blood flow patterns during echocardiography. The microbubbles oscillate in response to ultrasound waves, creating enhanced backscatter and improved image contrast.

Clinical Utility

Primary therapeutic applications include enhancement of left ventricular opacification and improvement of endocardial border delineation during echocardiography in patients with suboptimal acoustic windows.
