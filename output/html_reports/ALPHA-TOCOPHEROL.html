Source Investigation

ALPHA-TOCOPHEROL is derived from natural sources. Alpha-tocopherol is the most biologically active form of vitamin E, naturally occurring in various plant sources including wheat germ oil, sunflower seeds, almonds, hazelnuts, spinach, and other leafy green vegetables. It is also found in vegetable oils such as sunflower, safflower, and olive oil.
