Clinical Utility

Raloxifene is primarily indicated for the prevention and treatment of osteoporosis in postmenopausal women and for reduction of invasive breast cancer risk in postmenopausal women with osteoporosis or at high risk for breast cancer. The medication has a favorable safety profile compared to hormone replacement therapy, with lower risks of endometrial cancer and breast cancer.
