Source Investigation

LEVODOPA is derived from natural sources. Levodopa (L-DOPA, L-3,4-dihydroxyphenylalanine) occurs naturally in several plant species, most notably in Mucuna pruriens (velvet bean), where it comprises 3-6% of the seed weight. It is also found in Vicia faba (broad bean), seedlings of Vicia species, and other leguminous plants.
