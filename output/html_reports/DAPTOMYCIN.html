Clinical Utility

Daptomycin is indicated for complicated skin and soft tissue infections caused by gram-positive bacteria, including methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus (MRSA), and for Staphylococcus aureus bacteremia, including right-sided endocarditis.
