### Mechanism of Action Pentolinium acts as a competitive ganglionic blocking agent, inhibiting nicotinic acetylcholine receptors at autonomic ganglia. This results in blockade of both sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous system transmission, leading to significant hypotension through peripheral vasodilation and reduced cardiac output.
