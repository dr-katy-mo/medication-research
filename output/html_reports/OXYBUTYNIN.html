Clinical Utility

Primary therapeutic applications include treatment of overactive bladder, neurogenic bladder dysfunction, and urinary incontinence. Oxybutynin is available in immediate-release tablets, extended-release formulations, and transdermal patches to optimize dosing and reduce side effects.
