### Mechanism of Action Hydroxocobalamin acts as a vitamin B12 replacement and precursor to active cobalamin coenzymes. Once absorbed, it is converted to methylcobalamin and adenosylcobalamin, which serve as cofactors for methionine synthase and methylmalonyl-CoA mutase respectively.
