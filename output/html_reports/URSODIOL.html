URSODIOL is produced through modification of natural compounds, preserving the essential natural framework while enhancing therapeutic properties. Ursodiol (ursodeoxycholic acid, UDCA) occurs naturally as a secondary bile acid in human bile, constituting approximately 1-5% of total bile acids in healthy individuals.
