Bivalirudin is primarily used as an anticoagulant during percutaneous coronary interventions (PCI) and in patients with heparin-induced thrombocytopenia (HIT) undergoing PCI. The medication's short half-life (25 minutes) allows for rapid reversal of anticoagulant effects, making it particularly suitable for procedural use.
