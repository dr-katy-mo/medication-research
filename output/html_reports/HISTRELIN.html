### Mechanism of Action Histrelin acetate acts as a potent GnRH receptor agonist. Upon administration, it initially stimulates GnRH receptors, causing a transient increase in LH and FSH release. With continuous exposure, the receptors become desensitized and downregulated, leading to suppression of gonadotropin release and subsequent reduction in gonadal hormone production (testosterone in males, estradiol in females).
