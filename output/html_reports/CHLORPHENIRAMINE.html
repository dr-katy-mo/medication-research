### Mechanism of Action Chlorpheniramine acts as a competitive antagonist at histamine H1 receptors, preventing histamine from binding and activating downstream signaling cascades. This blocks histamine-induced vasodilation, increased vascular permeability, smooth muscle contraction, and sensory nerve activation.
