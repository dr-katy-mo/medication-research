Mechanism of Action

Erdafitinib is an ATP-competitive, reversible inhibitor of FGFR kinases. It binds to the ATP-binding site of FGFR1-4, preventing autophosphorylation and downstream signaling through pathways including RAS/RAF/MEK/ERK and PI3K/AKT. Erdafitinib is FDA-approved for the treatment of adults with locally advanced or metastatic urothelial carcinoma that has susceptible FGFR3 or FGFR2 genetic alterations and has progressed during or following platinum-containing chemotherapy.
