Mechanism of Action

Lubiprostone activates ClC-2 chloride channels in the apical membrane of gastrointestinal epithelial cells, leading to chloride efflux into the intestinal lumen. Clinical Utility

Lubiprostone is FDA-approved for chronic idiopathic constipation in adults, constipation-predominant irritable bowel syndrome (IBS-C) in women, and opioid-induced constipation in adults with chronic non-cancer pain.
