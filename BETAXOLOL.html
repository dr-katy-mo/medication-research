Betaxolol selectively blocks beta-1 adrenergic receptors, primarily in cardiac tissue, while having minimal effect on beta-2 receptors in bronchial and vascular smooth muscle. The medication offers cardiovascular protection through blood pressure reduction and heart rate control.
