Macimorelin functions as an orally active ghrelin mimetic that binds to and activates growth hormone secretagogue receptors in the anterior pituitary and hypothalamus. This results in a predictable increase in serum growth hormone levels in individuals with normal pituitary function.

Macimorelin is FDA-approved specifically for the diagnosis of adult growth hormone deficiency (AGHD).
