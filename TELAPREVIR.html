Current Status

Telaprevir was FDA-approved in May 2011 for hepatitis C treatment but was voluntarily withdrawn from the US market by the manufacturer (Vertex Pharmaceuticals) in October 2014. The withdrawal was due to decreased demand following availability of newer, more tolerable direct-acting antivirals. The medication is no longer available for clinical use in most countries.
